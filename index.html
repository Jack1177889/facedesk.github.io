NMNA 1914-1930

There used to be another company other than the one we have now. It was a car corps, but they were disbanded shortly after WWII. The car corps consisted of multiple cars that were all made by Royal Page Davidson. (Born: October 9, 1870; Died January 16, 1943) he made many different types of armored cars and had gone on extensive trips in said cars. He had made cadillacs into ballon destroyers with 50 cal brownings on them that could swivel horizontal and verticaly 180 degrees. One time, when he was driving these cars, he ran into a man who was forcing all of the cars on the road go through a ditch that he dug because he hated cars. But when the man saw NMNA driving their cars he let them drive around the ditch because he was scared of the guns mounted on their cars.

One of Corporal Davidson's most famous cars was his Cadillac Balloon Destroyer. He took the frame and engine of a Cadillac and added extensive parts, like a 50 cal browning that had been able to move vertical and horizontal, 180 degrees, which had made that a good reason to get them. The army had even started to enlist these vehicles during WWII because of how great they were wityh tracking on hard terrain. Sadly Corporal wasn't able to see this because he had passed away in 1943.
